\n

The forecasted temperature for the 700 millibar (10,000' - 11,000') level in our atmosphere is supposed to rise to 37-40\xb0F today. There will be plenty of warm air to get the snow surface damp/wet on all aspects and elevations. The wild card will be the cloud cover and the southerly winds, this may just keep the wet activity at bay and allow the snow surface to stay cold. However, if you're noticing the snow becoming damp and you're seeing pinwheels & roller balls cascading down the slope it's a sure bet the snow is warming and it's time to move to a colder aspect or lower your slope angle.
